Our CEO Thomas Beaumont

Thomas had always been captivated by the intricate world of timepieces, with their artistry and precision. His fascination led him on a journey to become a connoisseur of watches, appreciating their rich history and the craftsmanship behind each piece.

    After years of collecting and studying watches, Thomas noticed a gap in the market. While there were plenty of stores selling new luxury watches, there weren't many options for those who desired the charm and character of pre-owned timepieces. Inspired by his own experiences and a desire to share his passion with others, Thomas set out to establish a unique and trusted destination for used watches.
‍
   With a keen eye for quality and authenticity, Thomas embarked on a quest to source the finest pre-owned watches from around the world. He tirelessly built relationships with reputable suppliers, ensuring that each watch that entered his inventory met his high standards of craftsmanship, condition, and originality.

    As he cultivated his collection, Thomas pondered over the perfect name for his venture. He wanted a name that captured the essence of his store—a place where exceptional watches would be celebrated. One day, while sifting through old books on horology, he stumbled upon the term "Watchgoods." It resonated with him, encapsulating the notion of watches being more than just timekeeping devices but valuable goods that hold stories and memories.

   And so, Watchgoods was born. Under Thomas's leadership, the store quickly gained recognition for its curated selection of used watches, featuring renowned brands and sought-after models. Customers flocked to the store, drawn not only by the allure of luxury at more accessible prices but also by the authenticity and trust imbued in every purchase.

    Thomas's unwavering dedication to his craft and commitment to customer satisfaction set Watchgoods apart from the competition. He ensured that each customer received personalized attention, guiding them through the selection process and sharing his vast knowledge of horology. His passion for watches and desire to create a community of like-minded enthusiasts led him to establish events, workshops, and an online platform where customers could connect and learn more about their cherished timepieces.

    Today, Watchgoods stands as a testament to Thomas's vision and love for watches. It continues to thrive, providing a haven for watch enthusiasts, collectors, and those seeking a touch of elegance in their lives. As the CEO of Watchgoods, Thomas remains committed to offering the finest used watches, sharing the stories they carry, and spreading the joy and appreciation for horology with every customer who enters his store.
